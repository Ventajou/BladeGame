Bouncing ball

Here we simulate the inclusion of a game on a site with a fluid layout. The area available to the game will vary according to the size of the browser window or the device screen. Try resizing your browser or rotating your tablet to see the difference.

Many games are built with certain dimensions in mind, and can't adapt to the browser window like a website can. It is however possible to resize them as long as we preserve their proportions. In this example our game uses a resolution of 320 by 240 pixels internally
